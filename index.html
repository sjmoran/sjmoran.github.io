I'm currently an Executive Director (Head of Research) at JP Morgan Chase & Co. leading a Machine Learning R&D team. Previously I was a Senior Research Scientist at Huawei Technologies R&D and a member of the Edinburgh University NLP group.

My research focuses on traditional and deep learning machine learning methods for image, video and text understanding.
