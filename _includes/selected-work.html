CVPR 2020

DeepLPF

Deep local parametric filters that learn ellipse, gradient, and polynomial transforms for image enhancement. Interpretable and parameter-efficient; state-of-the-art on MIT-Adobe FiveK at the time of publication.

ICPR 2020

CURL — Neural Curve Layers

A small, differentiable colour-curve module that turns global tone-mapping into a learnable layer — competitive with much larger image-enhancement networks across three benchmarks.

Paper Code Talk
ECCV 2020

SIDGAN — Low-Light Video

A two-stage CycleGAN that synthesises low-light raw video for training enhancement networks in a setting where labelled data is unavailable.

FinLLM @ IJCAI 2023

SpamT5

Benchmarks several LLM families for few-shot email spam detection and introduces a fine-tuned Flan-T5 baseline. Released openly through JPMorgan with model weights and training code.

ISSREW 2025

CodeQUEST

An Evaluator/Optimizer framework that uses LLMs to score code along ten quality dimensions and iteratively improve it. Validated against established static-analysis proxies and used in an internal developer workflow.
